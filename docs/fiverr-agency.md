# Fiverr AI-Ads Agency — launch kit

Fiverr has no seller API. You create the account + gig and post replies yourself; the engine
handles fulfilment (Client Orders page → Generate 4K Ad) and drafts your replies.

## Gig title
"I will create a scroll-stopping AI UGC video ad for your product"

## Gig description
Stop paying £100s per UGC video. I create realistic, scroll-stopping AI video ads for e-commerce
brands, apps, restaurants and local businesses — cinematic product hero shots, UGC-style social
ads, and clean brand end-cards, delivered in crisp 4K.

Your real product, animated accurately (I anchor on your actual product photo so it looks exactly
like your product — not a hallucinated version). Fast turnaround, unlimited concepts on the
higher tiers, and platform-ready 9:16 / 1:1 / 16:9 exports.

What you get: a finished vertical video ad with music + sound design, ready for TikTok, Reels,
and Shorts. Send me your product photo + a one-line brief and I handle the rest.

## Packages (starting at cost while building reviews — raise after ~10 five-star reviews)
| Tier | Price | Delivery | Includes |
|---|---|---|---|
| BASIC | £15 | 2 days | 1× ~6s quick UGC ad, 4K, music + SFX, 9:16 |
| STANDARD | £35 | 2 days | 1× ~15s ad, 4K, music + SFX, 1 revision, 9:16 + 1:1 exports |
| PREMIUM | £75 | 3 days | 1× cinematic 15s ad + 3 hook/caption variants, all 3 formats, 2 revisions, commercial license |

Model cost per ad (Seedance 2.0 4K) ≈ £2–5, so even BASIC is profitable. "At cost" = keep prices low
to farm the first reviews, then raise.

1) your product photo (or a link), and 2) a one-line vibe/hook (e.g. "calm, cozy, for dog owners").
Which package fits — a quick 6s social ad or a full 15s spot? Happy to answer any questions.
